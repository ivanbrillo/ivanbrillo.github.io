I’m a Master’s student in Artificial Intelligence & Data Engineering at [University of Pisa](https://www.unipi.it), and an Honors student in Robotics at [Sant'Anna School of Advanced Studies](https://www.santannapisa.it). I hold a BSc in Computer Engineering, summa cum laude, from [University of Padua](https://www.unipd.it).

I apply AI to high-impact scientific and healthcare problems. My background covers machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, signal processing, and embedded systems, with hands-on research experience in multidisciplinary teams and collaborations with [Fondazione Monasterio](https://www.monasterio.it) and the [E. Piaggio Research Center](https://www.centropiaggio.unipi.it). I participated in the Formula Student team [RaceUP](https://raceup.it/), contributing to software and embedded systems development, and will soon spend four months in Montréal as a recipient of the [Mitacs Globalink Research Award](https://www.mitacs.ca/our-programs/globalink-research-award/) to develop surrogate models for computationally expensive Gaussian Process updates in neurostimulation.

I am open to research collaborations in healthcare, robotics, and biological systems.
